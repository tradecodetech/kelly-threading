## 3. Content Pillars (5 Core Topics)

Every post belongs to one of these five categories. Rotate through them to keep variety.

### Pillar 1 — TRANSFORMATION (40% of content)
Before & after content. The #1 highest-performing content type for beauty services.
- Eyebrow threading before/after (most shareable)
- Waxing result close-ups
- Facial glow results
- "Same client, 4 weeks apart" series
> Always get client permission before posting photos. Have a simple verbal consent or a small sign at checkout.

### Pillar 2 — EDUCATION (20% of content)
Build trust and position Kelly as the expert in Lubbock.
- "Threading vs. waxing — which is right for you?"
- "How often should you get your brows threaded?"
- "What is a Diamond Facial and who is it for?"
- "Bikini Line vs. Brazilian — what's the difference?"
- "Why you should never shave between threading appointments"
- Skin care tips after waxing

### Pillar 3 — BEHIND THE SCENES (15% of content)
Humanize the brand. People book with people they trust.
- Day-in-the-life at the salon
- "Meet your esthetician" intro post
- Setup shots, clean tools, product walkthrough
- Prep routine before opening

### Pillar 4 — PROMOTIONS & OFFERS (15% of content)
Drive bookings directly. Keep promo code KELLY10 active.
- Monthly specials (see Section 6)
- "Book today, same-day appointments available"
- Combo deal posts (e.g., Eyebrows + Lip + Chin = Full Face $35)
- Birthday month discount posts
- Referral offer: "Bring a friend, both get 10% off"

### Pillar 5 — SOCIAL PROOF (10% of content)
Reviews, testimonials, re-shares.
- Screenshot and repost Google/Facebook reviews (with permission)
- "Customer of the week" spotlight
- Re-share client tagged posts
- Milestone posts ("500 clients served!" etc.)

## 7. Reels / TikTok Video Ideas

Short-form video is the #1 organic reach driver in 2026. Start simple — phone camera is fine.

1. **Threading close-up** — 15 sec, satisfying thread pull on brows, no talking needed
2. **"How long does it take?"** — 30 sec, quick walkthrough of a full brow service
3. **Before/after reveal** — slow zoom out, before photo → after photo with music
4. **"What's a Diamond Facial?"** — talking head, 30 sec explanation
5. **"Walk-ins welcome today"** — Saturday morning, 10 sec, show the open salon
6. **Price list breakdown** — text overlay on calming background music
7. **Client reaction** — get clients (with permission) to react to their results
8. **"A day at Kelly's"** — 30–60 sec montage of the full salon day

## 8. Review Generation Strategy

Reviews are the #1 trust signal for local businesses. Goal: **20+ Google reviews in 60 days.**

**Process:**
1. After every service, verbally say: *"If you loved your experience, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us."*
2. Have a QR code at the checkout counter that links directly to your Google review page
3. Text or DM the booking link with a review request 2 hours after their appointment
4. Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours

**Response templates:**
- Positive: *"Thank you so much [Name]! We loved having you in and can't wait to see you again. — Kelly's Team"*
- Negative: *"We're sorry to hear this. Please call us at (806) 281-0650 so we can make it right."*
